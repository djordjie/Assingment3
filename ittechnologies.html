Autonomous vehicles

What does it do?

The current best available example of this technology is the Tesla Model S which is not completely autonomous and still requires a driver to always have their hands on the steering wheel. The features of this car are autopilot and enhanced autopilot. Best self-driving cars 2022 | heycar 2022, Heycar.co.uk, viewed 30 March 2022, . The features included in autopilot are traffic-aware cruise control which detects the speed of the nearby cars and sets your speed accordingly. The other feature is autosteer: which keeps the car driving within two marked lines on either side. The enhanced autopilot consists of auto lane change, auto park, summon and smart summon. Auto lane change aids the driver when merging onto freeways or highways. Auto Park, as the name suggests aids in parking, particularly for perpendicular and parallel spaces. Summon aids in parking and leaving small park spaces. Smart summon is a feature that allows you to send your car from its parking spot directly to your stationary position assuming you are in the direct vicinity. Autopilot and Full Self-Driving Capability 2020, Tesla.com, viewed 30 March 2022, .

Another benefit is that flaws can be revealed, and improvements can be made to improve current and future autonomous vehicles. In addition, a way to improve autonomous vehicles is by improving our roads and infrastructure related to driving so that autonomous vehicles can easily detect road lanes, traffic lights, signs, and potential obstacles. To make autonomous vehicles more mainstream other car companies should invest in similar technology to Tesla leading to more competition and more options for the consumer which makes the costs lower to purchase an autonomous vehicle increasing the potential for middle-class people to purchase them. This encourages investment in the technology leading to faster developments and improvement. In combination with this, the stigma surrounding the safety of autonomous vehicles must be discussed and reduced or eliminated. Otherwise, the developments are pointless if people do not feel safe, they will always use an alternative.

This results in more automated processes and less human interference required. With the continual development of radar sensors, light detection and ranging sensors, ultraviolet sensors, and video camera technology. Raspberry Pis, Arduinos, Makey Makeys and other small computing devices 

What does it do? 

A raspberry pi is a microprocessor whereas an Arduino is a microcontroller. A significant difference is that a raspberry pi requires an operating system for it to work. Whereas an Arduino only requires code uploaded to run. There is no single state of the art computing device out of those above since they all have different purposes. What are the differences between Raspberry Pi and Arduino? 2021, Electronics Hub, viewed 1 April 2022, . The state-of-the-art Raspberry Pi is the Raspberry Pi 4 Model B which has two USB (Universal Serial Bus) 2.0 ports and 2 USB 3.0 ports. A gigabit of ethernet. 2 micro-HDMI ports which support 4k displays. A USB-c power supply. Raspberry Pi Ltd 2022, Buy a Raspberry Pi 4 Model B – Raspberry Pi, Raspberry Pi, viewed 1 April 2022, . The raspberry pi can be used to run a computer or a retro video game emulator. The best Arduino board is the Arduino Uno Wi-Fi Rev 2 which has an operating voltage of 5v, recommended input voltage is 7-12v it has 14 pins 5 of which are PWM, 6 input pins, 20mA for the input/output pins and 50mA for the 3.3vpin. ARDUINO UNO WiFi REV2 2021, Arduino Online Shop, viewed 1 April 2022, . Arduino can be used for making robotic arms or toy cars. A Makey Makey is a circuit board that allows connector pins to be connected to objects and a computer. The objects can be used for programs created on the computer or work as a button. Makey Makey 2012, Joylabz Official Makey Makey Store, viewed 6 April 2022, . Other small computing devices have similar uses as the Arduino or Raspberry pi. Clouds, services and servers

What does it do? 

The cloud refers to the access of servers through the internet and the software and databases that run on them. Using a provider and the internet physical servers can be accessed from anywhere at any time, allowing users to interact and connect with Infrastructure, platforms, and software that they would not normally have access to. It allows users to store information on servers rather than their hardware. This can be used for storage of data on a server which is only limited by the amount that the user is willing to pay for. It also gives a neutral location to store data allowing for safer and easier collaboration. Along with access to subscription-based software, Such as OneDrive. 

The current state of Cloud computing allows for organizations and individuals to easily configure the structure to allow for easy handling of applications. Which allows for an array of services to be provided, such as the access to Infrastructure, Platforms, and software. This is done by a provider and works as a pay-per-use system, so you only pay for what you desire. In its current state it allows for flexibility and easy scalability to increase or decrease the system depending on the users demands. This has allowed for increased developer productivity, increased application performance and flexible global scaling. 

The current applications of cloud computing cover many areas such as testing and development, storage, disaster recovery, infrastructure, and platforms. Firstly, testing and development has been improved dramatically with the ability to acquire already made environments which can be tailored to suit the user’s needs, this elevates the need for physical assets, time, and unneeded effort. Secondly cloud storage allows users to store and access data on or off premises, using a third-party provider, which allows for storage without the need for infrastructure and security. Thirdly it allows for cost effective disaster recovery, thanks to cloud-based backups and recovery procedures put in place by the providers. Lastly infrastructure and platforms allow for subscription-based services, which works as a pay-as-you-go process giving users access to IT infrastructure and platforms for developing, managing, and running applications. 

The probable future of cloud computing is the expansion of the accessibility of more information. It is most likely that improvement and expansion will be done in the areas that already utilize cloud computing such as health care, education, and marketing. Cloud computing has been applied in healthcare in the form of digital records which can be accessed from almost any clinic, it can be expected that cloud computing will become more prominent in this field in the coming years with the increase of digital formatting. It is also probable that education will continue down the path of cloud computing, with the normalization of remote learning and digital libraries. Marketing will be another area which will further normalize cloud computing with the increase of online shopping, which will help improve marketing strategies and increase customer interaction. 

The main technology that has enabled cloud computing is virtualization, which allows for the one physical computer to create additional virtual system which can be used to create, manage, and perform tasks. This allowed companies to sell access to these systems over the internet so that users can have access to hardware that is in a different location. This gave access to company’s and users that needed/wanted a system but did not want to own and maintain the hardware, so they decided it was safer or more affordable to outsource. This alongside the increased demand for space, security and affordability in the digital world resulted in what is known as ‘The Cloud’ or cloud computing.

What is the likely impact? 

The impact that cloud computing could have on a business level would be the reduction of cost and flexibility of the systems. Cloud computing works with a pay-per-use system so business will not have to use as much money to maintain their systems. The flexibility of cloud computing allows a comfortable fit for any business who needs a system as it can be tailored to the needed specifications. Security and disaster recovery will be increased, and resources can be scaled as need be to suit the needed desire. It will have effects on Health care, education, and shopping as they become more digital, as it allows for better collaboration and sharing of data, along with accessibility, for users and businesses. 

The people who will be most affected with the change to the cloud would be everyone from, business owners, workers, and users. Such as teachers and how they will teach content from anywhere without difficulty. Students will be able to access their work, content, software, and digital libraries from anywhere, allowing students to learn without exception. It will also allow for the common individual to store data , such as photos, documents, programs, in secure locations without the need for bulky hardware. It will improve the healthcare system, by allowing any clinics to see your records and information, without the need for tedious transfers. 

This will decrease the need for personal IT professionals within business as they transition to the cloud as it will remove the need for maintenance and upkeep of their systems or servers that would become obsolete by transferring to a cloud computing system. This does not mean that IT professionals will become obsolete within the business, but it will encourage them to change the way in which they work for them.

 Machine Learning

What does it do?

Machine learning is a form of artificial intelligence which allows programs and software applications to predict outcomes to increase its accuracy. This allows computers to act without having to explicitly program it. Machine learning is conducted to solve problems with speed and scale which otherwise could not be achieved by a human, also allowing for one or multiple tasks to be operated at a time. Through the algorithms of machine learning and access to data patterns and relationships can be identified and altered to optimize the desired outcome. It is mostly used to predict new values from the use of historical data. The algorithms will create an estimate about the data, it is then evaluated to test the accuracy of the prediction, the process is then repeated to test whether a new data point is more suited. 

Current applications of machine learning are currently being used in many fields such as Health care, Retail, and transportation. Health care uses machine learning to analyze data allowing trends to be identified which has improved the diagnosis of issues, which is being used to keep track of increase and decrease in certain illnesses. Retail uses machine learning to identify trends between users by analyzing their purchase history to anticipate what they are likely to buy in the future. Machine learning is also currently being used in the transportation sector as the identification of popular routes is used to decide where new roads should be built and where old ones should be repaired. 

With the current technology, machine learning can be used for video games, internet search engines and speech recognition. Machine learning can be used to teach programs to play games, such as chess or checkers which allows for people to play against the computer, it is also commonly used within video games as well such as an enemy predicting where the player will move to after considering their past movement. It is used when searching on the internet, where it uses extraction to give a prediction of what you are wanting to search depending on what you have typed already and your past searches. A more complex form of machine learning is teaching a program to identify human speech for services such as Siri and Alexa. 

The main prediction for machine learning is that it will be more heavily applied to computer vision, which allows for computers to observe the world around them and understand what it is. This technology is already in use today with facial recognition, but it is currently not evolved enough to identify between objects. For example, the current systems find it difficult to identify between animals as they are complex shapes, this becomes even more difficult when they system tries to identify the breed of the animal as the system does not have enough data identify it properly. This will be overcome in time with the constant optimization of the technology and the increase in access to data. 

Machine learning is based on the interaction of brain cells which was demonstrated by a model in a book written by Donald Hebb in 1949 called ‘The Organization of Behavior’ which covered the communication between neurons. The principle that was proposed was put into action by Arthur Samuel who in the early 1950’s developed a computer program which could play chess against him. Due to the limited memory the computer had it could only anticipate one move in advance, but over time the program evolved into a minimax algorithm. This was the proof of concept that the scientific community needed to start the advancement of machine learning. 

What is the likely impact? 

The potential impacts of machine learning are the improvement in health care, improved business strategies and automation. Businesses will have access to the technology to understand and predict users' interests and shopping habits and allow them to target people with promotion and advertising. This will increase productivity and effectiveness by finding the optimal solution for the given problem but potentially at the cost of human interaction.

Users of social media and internet services will be affected by the retail aspect of machine learning as companies learn about their shopping patterns and habits, giving them more accurate advertising opportunities. Everyone will be positively impacted by the identification of medical patterns which could identify the cause and effect of certain actions and issues, which will help the community become a healthier place. 

This will create jobs for programmers who will develop and manage the technology, in whatever field it is being used it. Along with added jobs for analysts who will look at the patterns and data created by the technology and determine if and what it should be used for. There will be a negative side to this as more and more advancements are made in machine learning, it will slowly take away from other areas which it seeks to help as it removes the need for teams of people working on problems the technology could work much fast and with greater success.
