Skills and jobs

Room 15 is a website for the centralized sharing of user-created or already existing horror stories based on real-life experiences, we are looking to hire four members of a development team, to develop the website idea for a further six months. For the full development of the website, this team would require a Graphic designer for the creation of visual assets, a Front-end developer for the writing of the user experience code, a Back-end developer for the writing of the background code and a Chief financial officer for managing of the projects funding and monetization of the final website.

Room 15 is looking for a versatile and talented Graphics designer to create digital assets for our Web project, applicants would work closely with all other developers in the team on the creation of assets across all aspects of the company. An applicant would require at least three years of commercial design experience, at least one of those years needs to be a Web-based Graphic Design position and would require previous extensive experience with high-density teamwork environments. The applicant would oversee the creation of website UI, Borders, icons and the development of a consistent horror-themed style across the service, established with all other members of the team, that would be adhered to through development as well as the creation of advertisements for our services.

Any applicants must deliver consistent, high-quality material by semi-flexible deadlines. Applicants should have a good understanding of recent web design trends. All applicants must be excellent team players, be good at taking constructive feedback, have good verbal and written communication skills, hand illustrative abilities and understand basic IT jargon and terminology for smooth work with other developers. An understanding of Microsoft teams, Word, PowerPoint, and excel for any online collaborations is required. Any applicants must be qualified for Graphics design via a university degree or similar and have extensive experience and technical proficiency with the Adobe Creative Suite.

For the implementation of Web assets and their further development for efficiency and performance, Room 15 is hiring a Front-end web developer. Front-end developers will work consistently with a Back-end developer and Graphics designer to bring a completed version of Room 15’s creative vision to fruition. Applicants would work closely with all other members of the development team on ensuring the timely delivery of new versions of the website through collaborative tools such as GitHub as well as working closely with a Back-end developer to ensure the security of the website’s information, such as user login data.

All applicants require at least three years of Front-end developer experience and previous experience in social media design. Applicants are required to be able to develop tidy, organised and well-documented code for ease of new feature development as well as an understanding of MVC systems. A strong understanding of JavaScript, HTML, CSS, GIT for version control, C#, Microsoft Visual Studio and an excellent understanding of information architecture and security is required.

It is expected that Front-end developers will have a basic understanding of the Adobe Creative Cloud to facilitate smooth collaboration with our Graphics Designer and a good understanding of SQL databases for collaboration with Back-end developers. It is positive to have a good understanding of recent web trends as well as a love for horror.

All applicants must be able to work well in a team when under pressure and collaborate well both in-person and online utilizing programs such as GitHub, Microsoft teams and Office 365. All applicants are required to have a university degree in Computer Science or a related field.

Back-end developers are key in web development, including in the development of Horror story-based social media such as Room 15. we are seeking out applicants to facilitate the development of SQL based databases and other back-end technologies for Room 15's development. Back-end developers will work closely with the development team to design any tools or products to facilitate the development of the website.

Utilizing their experience with several coding languages Back-end developers will develop and deploy high-quality databases to store user information, horror story ratings, comments, and references to outside media. Working closely with Front-end developers all applicants will ensure the quality of the website meets market standards, including developing optimizations for load time, reactivity and data security protocols.

An extremely strong work ethic and excellent collaboration skills are required, in addition to the ability to make tidy and easy to read code for collaboration with all developers and ease of upgradeability. All applicants are required to have extensive technical knowledge of SQL, C#, C++, .NET, GIT, database design and computer security. A strong understanding of Microsoft Teams, GitHub and office 365 is required for proper team collaboration. A University level degree in software engineering or computer sciences is a requirement for all applicants. Room 15 is developing a horror-based social media which will have to handle investments into its developments and as such is looking to hire a Chief Financial Officer.

Our CFO will oversee all finances from all departments to ensure efficient spending, manage relations with stakeholders and be consistently consulting with the CEO to prepare financial models. The CFO’s key role in the company would be to raise further capital to allow for continued steady development by researching market trends to evaluate several forms of monetization such as advertisements and subscription services, ensure compliance with all financial laws, consult with external stakeholders and managing an annual and monthly budget. The CFO will be working closely with other members of the development team such as the Front-end developer for advertisement and the CEO for major managerial decisions regarding the company’s future spending.

Applicants are required to have professional qualifications in accounting such as a bachelor’s degree. Previous experience in a senior managerial role is required including previous experience in IT. Excellent communication skills are required to properly collaborate with both internal development team members as well as external partners and stakeholders. All applicants require excellent analytical and organisational skills and the ability to identify financial opportunities and explore the associated risks and benefits.
