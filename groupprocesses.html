Group Processes

After the completion of assignment two we learnt a lot. At the beginning everything was going well we decisively allocated work to each other. There were some issues relating to the attendance of the meetings. Some people had emergencies and issues that arise that could not be avoided. We were also flexible with the meeting times to assure the maximum number of members could attend the following meeting. An issue that created lots of friction and disagreement was the late submission of drafts which caused unnecessary stress for the other members and arguments which damages the trust people have with each other. Another issue was the lack of communication sometimes which led to people not knowing exactly what was expected next meeting. Overall, we learnt a lot from each other which will allow us to work better together in this assignment. To rectify some of these problems we commit to having more open communication with each other to assure we are aware of any issues a member may be having completing their section of the assignment. So, there is time for other members to help or complete the work for them if necessary. Another measure we are taking is asking for weekly updates on work that has been done so that members are aware of each other’s progress of their designated sections and to stop potential cramming of work at the end. To improve communication and transparency we will also try our best to be more punctual for each meeting leading to more productive meetings and an increase in morale for the group.
